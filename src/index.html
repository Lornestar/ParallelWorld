Parallel world cup is an Ethereum based sports betting application, that lets you bet on the Soccer world cup by purchasing competiing teams. Own the team that wins the world cup and you win the entire pot.


One Owner Per Team

Only one person can own a team at one time


Rebuy Teams

Anyone can buy any team at a price higher than the last person purchased


20% Rebuy Comissions

If someone buys a team from you, you get your ETH back plus 20% of the additional ETH paid


Pooled Funds

Most of the ETH paid goes into the pot


Winner Takes All

The owner of the winning world cup team wins all the ETH in the pot


Ends July 15 7:45

Team buying ends July 15 7:45pm Moscow time, which is the estimated time the world cup winner will be known

FAQs

What do I need to play?

  • Metamask extension installed
  • Ether in your metamask wallet to purchase teams

How do I install metamask?

Visit https://metamask.io/ for instructions.

How do I get Ether into metamask?

The most common way is to buy Ether through a cryptocurrency exchange such as Coinbase or Bitstamp. After you own Ether, transfer the ether to your MetaMask wallet.

When someone buys a team, how is the ETH distributed?

If it's the first time someone purchased that team:
  • 90% goes to the pot
  • 10% goes to the developer of this game

If someone has purchased the team before:
  • The previous owner gets the amount they paid plus 20% of the difference in the new price
  • 10% of the difference in new price goes to the developer
  • 70% of the difference goes into the pot

Can I prevent others from my buying my team ?

No. Anyone can purchase your team away from you until July 15 7:45pm Moscow time.

How do I know this game is not rigged ?

All team purchases are shown on the public Ethereum blockchain. You can audit every transaction.
Also, you can audit the smart contract which keeps track of all transactions, and decides who gets all the money.
